Inbound

Features

Our inbound call center software gives smart routing to your callers so that they get connected with the right agent based on skill set.This
increase first contact resolution rate and help in increasing conversion rate too in sales processes.

Queue Management and Call Routing

DNIS Based queue:

The route calls based on the DNIS to specific campaigns, skillsets and agents. The
system also allows to define the wildcard/prefix to be configured to call routing.

Priority queue:

Priority landing of calls to the agent can be configured by priorizing the queue calls
based on the customer metedata, call history or based on the selections on the IVR.

CLI Based call routing:

VIP or priority customers can be routed separtely by the system to deliver the privilged
service.

Skillset based Routing:

Calls can be routed to specific group of agents on their skillset.In case there are
multi-skill agents. the system delivers the call based on the priority of the agent skill.

ACD

A from of ACD is now must a typical contact center.
Having a system in place that automatically routes caller to
relevant individuals cuts down on wasted time and resources
ACD systems distribute calls accroding to user-specified
criteria, such as sending to call to phone that is idle or
sending calls about billing to the accounting department etc.
Nearly all contact center system have some from ACD
They vary from simple push-button vitual operators to those
that use caller id or voice voice-recognition.
